Shoulder Workout

Arnold Press

Arnold Press helps to strengthen your shoulders!
For an effective workout, make sure to:
  • Stand straight up with legs spread at shoulder length.
  • Let the barbell hang at arm's length in front of you.
  • Slowly lift the bar upwards to your chest while keeping your upper-arms still.
  • Remember to keep your back straight and to not swing the weight as it will make it easier for you to lift the weight.
