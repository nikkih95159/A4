Arm Workout - biceps

Hammer curls w/ rope

Rope Hammer curls are important for your biceps.
For an effective workout, make sure to:
  • Stand up straight.
  • Put the rope up in front of you with both sides of the rope in each hand.
  • Pull the rope up to your lower chest level.
  • Make sure not to move your elbows around.
  • Make sure to use a weight suitable for you.
