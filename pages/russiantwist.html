Ab Workout

Russian Twists

Russian twists are important in building up your abs.
For an effective workout, make sure to:
  • Sit on the floor with your legs togethers and slightly extended above the ground.
  • Put your hands together with your elbows elevated.
  • Twist to the right putting your right elbow to the ground.
  • Alternate sides and make sure your elbows touch the floor.
