Arm Workout

Preacher Curl

(Duration: -- min)
Preacher Curls help build up the lower part of your biceps!
For an effective workout, make sure to:
  • Sit so that the pad is slanted away from you.
  • Put your upper arms in front of the pad and your chest against the back.
  • Firmly grip the barbell and slowly bring it towards you.
  • Don't bend your wrists and make sure to use manageable weights.
