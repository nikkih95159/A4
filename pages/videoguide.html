Arm Workout

#1 Preacher Curl

(Duration: 10 min)
Preacher Curls help build up the lower part of your bicep! To begin Preacher Curls:
  1. Sit on the machine so that the pad is slanted away from you.
  2. Put your upper arms in front of the pad and your chest against the back
  3. Firmly grip the barbell and this is you starting position!
