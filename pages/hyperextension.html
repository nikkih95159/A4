Back Workout - lower back

Hyper extensions

Hyper extensions are important in building up your lower back.
For an effective workout, make sure to:
  • Lay at an angle with your lower body resting on the seat.
  • Lower your upper body towards the ground with your hands crossed over on your chest.
  • Pull up your upper body once it goes fully down.
  • Make sure you only use your lower back to pull yourself up.
  • You can also place a weight in your hands to make the exercise harder.
