Chest Workout

Cable Fly

Cable Fly is important for strengthening your chest.
For an effective workout, make sure to:
  • Stand up straight.
  • Hold one cable in each hand and take a step forward.
  • Push the handles away from your body until your arm is extended.
  • Let both your hands meet and then bring the weight slowly back.
  • Make sure to use a weight suitable for you.
