Shoulder workout

Rear delt flys

Rear delt flys are important in building up your shoulders.
For an effective workout, make sure to:
  • Stand up and lean forward until your upper torso is almost parallel with the ground.
  • With a dumbbell in each hand, extend your arms from your sides like you are flapping wings.
  • Make sure to pull the weight up slowly.
  • Make sure to use a weight suitable for you.
