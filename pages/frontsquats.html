Leg Workout

Front Squats

Front squats are great for building up your legs.
For an effective workout, make sure to:
  • Stand up straight with your feet a little past shoulder length.
  • Place the barbell resting on two fingers or more of both of your hands.
  • Rest the barbell at around collarbone level using your fingers to hold it level.
  • Slowly bring yourself down with your weight driven in your heels.
  • Make sure to go parallel to the ground or lower than parallel.
  • Make sure to use suitable weights.
